Interactive Wireframe Cube, using Euler angles

Quaternion Cube

Controls

Rotate cube (from its perspective) - shift to reverse:
  • R: Roll (rotate around Z)
  • P: Pitch (rotate around X)
  • Y: Roll (rotate around Y)
Move cube (from its perspective):
  • Left/Right arrows: left/right (X-axis strafe)
  • Up/Down arrows: forward/back (Z-axis slide)
  • PgUp/PgDown: up/down (Y-axis strafe)
Move Camera View:
  • Mouse click+drag: aim camera crosshairs
  • W/S: Forward/Back
  • A/D: Strafe Left/Right
  • Z: Zoom In (shift-Z Out)
Note: the browser may capture some keys for page scrolling (eg Arrows, PgUp/Down). Using browser fullscreen (eg F11?) may help.
